Summary

Dr. Thomas Weinandy is an applied economist conducting research on a mobile two-sided promotions market. He specializes in business economics and digital economics, particularly in managing complex and big data sets. His professional work identifies insights on consumer behavior at brick-and-mortar retail amid macroeconomic trends. Dr. Weinandy also performs pro bono consulting on nonprofit program evaluation.
