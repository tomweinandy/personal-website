Three Types of AI Found in the Wild

  • Ideal audience: Non-technical, practitioners, students
  • Recommended length: 30-60min

Have you ever stepped back to ask what AI exactly is? There's a lot of talk about how AI is transforming businesses and the world, and although this is true, many people could benefit from a more fundamental understanding of how it works. I break down the three types of AI found in everyday settings and explain the distinct ways they can solve problems in life or business.

I clarify common misconceptions, answering why:

  • No, not all AI is the same
  • No, it's not like in the movies
  • No, AI isn't about to take everyone's jobs
