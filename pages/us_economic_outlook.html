The U.S. Economic Outlook

  • Ideal audience: Business managers and leaders
  • Recommended length: 30-60min

Here I explore the state of the U.S. economy and the forces shaping its next chapter. This is tailored to the audience but usually examines the classics: inflation, interest rates, household finances, and consumer sentiment, alongside current events like tariffs, geopolitics, and policy. (Don’t worry — although I may discuss relevant business policy, I remain politically neutral.) The focus is on what's relevant and what's next.

The talk unravels economic mysteries such as:

  • If consumers are so pessimistic, why do they keep spending?
  • Which headwinds and tailwinds are most likely to change the economy tomorrow?
  • What are the odds of a recession happening this year?
